About Me

After working as an administrative assistant for several years, I realized that my true passion lay in web development. I began teaching myself coding in my free time and eventually I enrolled in a coding bootcamp and quickly proved to be a skilled and dedicated learner. I became proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and several programming languages, and honed my skills in responsive web design, database management, and server-side programming. I credit my background in administration for giving me strong organizational skills and attention to detail, which I apply to my work as a developer.
